FUN WITH MICKEY IN DISNEYLAND, ANAHEIM, SOUTHERN CALIFORNIA.

Disneyland park is a tourist attraction because this place has too many interesting activities for all walks of life and age especially children. Among the places that attract tourist are meeting Disney character, Disney California adventure park, downtown Disney district, roller coaster, guardians of the galaxy and star wars galaxy's edge. We need to buy a ticked to enter the Disney park and we can go to all the interesting places in Disney park. The ticket can be used within a day and if we leave the park, we can still enter it because our ticket can still be used.
