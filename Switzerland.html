EXPLORING SWITZERLAND

THE MATTERHORN MOUNTAIN

The Matterhorn, Switzerland's iconic pointed peak is one of the highest mountains in the Alps. On the border with Italy, this legendary peak rises to 4,478 meters, and its four steep faces lie in the direction of the compass points.

SWISS GRAND CANYON

The Ruinaulta (also known as the "Swiss Grand Canyon") is a deep george surrounded by expansive meadows and forested cliffs. Today, the Swiss Grand Canyon is not only one of the most beautiful areas in Switzerland, but also a preffered destination for hikers, bird-watchers and nature lovers.

GRUYERES

Even if you don't know anything about this small medieval town, you've probably heard about the hard yellow cheese that made its name famous. Today, that's one of the town's main attractions. Visitors can tour a cheese factory, sample the local specialities, and wave to the cows that call the surrounding green hills home.
